Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes! All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your computer and are not stored on any server.

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG (with transparency), WebP (modern format), and JPEG. You can compress each format individually or convert between them.

How much can I reduce file sizes?

Typically 40-80% reduction depending on the original image and compression settings. WebP format usually provides the best compression.

Is there a file size limit?

Maximum 10MB per image and up to 20 images per session. This ensures fast processing and optimal performance.

Do you support transparency?

Yes! PNG and WebP formats fully support transparency preservation. JPEG does not support transparency.

Is this service really free?

Absolutely! ImageCompress is completely free with no hidden costs, registration requirements, or usage limits.
