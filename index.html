Help Guide

Step 1: Upload Images

Drag and drop your images or click to select files. You can upload up to 20 images at once. Supported formats: PNG, JPEG, WebP.

Step 2: Adjust Settings

Choose compression quality, maximum width, and output format according to your needs. Use tooltips for guidance.

Step 3: Compress

Click 'Start Compression' to process your images. The compression happens locally in your browser - your files never leave your computer.

Step 4: Download

Download individual images or all compressed files as a ZIP archive. Each download includes compression statistics.

Pro Tips:

  • Use WebP format for best compression with transparency support
  • Set max width to reduce dimensions for web use (recommended: 1200-2000px)
  • Quality 70-80% usually provides good balance between size and quality
  • All processing happens locally - your images never leave your computer
  • Use PNG format when you need transparency preservation
